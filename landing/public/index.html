How we can help

Our Missing Persons team assists potential applicants throughout Manitoba by collecting case information, sharing resources, and connecting families to dedicated case workers. If you are looking for someone you love, please reach out — a coordinator will follow up within one business day.

  • Application intake & safety planning
  • Coordination with First Nations leadership
  • Links to counselling and land-based supports

Need immediate assistance?

Call AMC Missing Persons at 204-555-0100 or email missing@manitobachiefs.com.

For emergencies, please contact your local police service or 911.
