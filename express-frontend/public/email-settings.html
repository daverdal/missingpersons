About Bounce-Back Emails

Where do bounces go? Bounce-back emails go to the "From Address" you specify above, not the Reply-To address.

Current protection: The system automatically filters out bounce-back emails from the Offender News inbox, so they won't trigger SMS alerts even if they arrive in your inbox.

Best practice: If you want to completely separate bounces, create a dedicated Gmail account (e.g., missingpersons-blasts@yourdomain.com) and use it as the "From Address". All bounces will go to that account instead of your main inbox.
